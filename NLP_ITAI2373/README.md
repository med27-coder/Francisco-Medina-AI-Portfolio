## About Me & My Interest in NLP

Language is the hardest thing to teach a machine — not because words are complex, but because meaning is. ITAI 2373 showed me how NLP transforms raw text into structured knowledge: from cleaning and vectorizing text, to engineering prompts that produce professional-grade outputs, to building agents that reason and remember across a conversation. This course connected the theory of language models to the practice of building real tools with them.
