## About Me & My Interest in Data Science

Data science is where statistics, programming, and domain knowledge intersect to produce decisions — and ITAI 2377 showed me that the most important work happens before any model runs. Preprocessing choices, feature engineering decisions, and data integrity checks determine whether a model learns real patterns or just memorizes noise. This course grounded my AI skills in the practical discipline of working with messy, real-world data across every modality: tabular records, text corpora, images, video, and audio.
